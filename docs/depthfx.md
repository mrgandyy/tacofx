# DepthFX

DepthFX is the “3D space” family: raymarch-inspired volumetric looks and depth-oriented effects.

## What it’s good at

- atmospheric volumes
- beam/ring/sweep effects
- “holographic fog” style layers
- depth-based distortion looks

## Hue shift controls

If an effect hue shifts automatically, it should ideally offer:
- local Hue Shift toggle
- Hue Speed control

This is especially important for raymarch-style effects, where forced hue shifting can clash with a world’s palette.

## Comfort and safety

DepthFX can get intense fast:
- avoid sharp strobing
- prefer smoothing/attack-release when driven by AudioLink
- keep very high contrast patterns away from full-screen coverage
